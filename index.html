CheeratAIBot Trading Engine

Designed to trade automatically, adapt to market conditions, and prioritize capital preservation.

OHLCV (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume)
Short-term price volatility
Market activity & liquidity signals
Trend-Following Strategy
  • Uses Moving Averages (MA, EMA)
  • Identifies bullish & bearish trends
  • Prioritizes trades with dominant market direction
  • Suitable for trending markets
Mean Reversion Strategy
  • Uses RSI & price deviation from averages
  • Identifies overbought & oversold conditions
  • Trades toward equilibrium
  • Effective in sideways markets
Volume-Based Strategy
  • Analyzes volume spikes & anomalies
  • Confirms trend strength
  • Filters weak/low-liquidity signals
Breakout Strategy
  • Detects consolidation zones
  • Triggers trades on confirmed breakouts
  • Uses volume confirmation to reduce false signals
AI Decision Layer
  • Assigns confidence scores to each strategy
  • Applies weighted decision-making
  • Produces final action: BUY, SELL, HOLD
